About this site

This ant keeping guide is meant to pull good, practical information into one place.

Each species page focuses on the things keepers care about most: temperature, humidity, diapause, founding style, setups by colony size, diet, and common issues.

The nuptial flight and forum sections help the community share what actually works in the real world.

This project was built with Django, Bootstrap, and a small swarm of virtual ants.
